Slither.io

Slither.io is a free online multiplayer game where players control a snake-like avatar, navigating a dark space to consume glowing orbs and grow in size. The objective is to become the largest snake on the server by outlasting and outmaneuvering other players. If a player's snake collides with another, they die and leave behind orbs for others to consume. The game combines simple mechanics with competitive strategy, making it highly addictive and easy to pick up.

Little Big Snake

A multiplayer snake game similar to Slither.io, but with added missions, an upgrade system, and more detailed, colorful graphics.

Snake (Nokia Classic)

A simple, retro game where you guide a pixelated snake to eat dots and grow longer while avoiding running into walls or your own tail.

WormsZone.io

An arcade-style online game where you control a worm, collect food, and battle others for size and survival, with customizable skins and power-ups.

Paper.io

A territory-claiming game inspired by snake mechanics, where players draw loops to expand their area while avoiding being cut off by opponents.
